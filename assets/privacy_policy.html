Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

Introduction

Rating Tracker ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Usernames

We store your competitive programming platform usernames (LeetCode, Codeforces, CodeChef) locally on your device. These usernames are used solely to fetch your public profile data from the respective platforms.

Local Storage

All data including usernames, settings, and cached ratings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.

Platform Data

We fetch your public rating data from LeetCode, Codeforces, and CodeChef APIs. We do not store this data on our servers; it is only temporarily cached on your device.

Device Information

We may collect basic device information for crash reporting and app stability improvements through Expo.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display your competitive programming ratings and progress within the App
  • Send you contest reminders based on your selected preferences
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and maintain app stability

Data Security

Your data security is important to us. All data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features. We do not use encryption for local storage as it remains on your personal device.

Support Us Feature & Financial Data

The App includes a voluntary "Support Us" feature to help fund development. This feature simply redirects you to third-party UPI applications already installed on your device. We do not collect, process, transmit, or store any of your financial information, bank details, or transaction history. All payment processing is handled entirely and securely by the external UPI application you select.

Third-Party Services

Rating Tracker uses the following third-party services:

  • LeetCode API - for fetching LeetCode user data
  • Codeforces API - for fetching Codeforces user data
  • CodeChef API - for fetching CodeChef user data
  • Expo - for push notifications and crash reporting

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Data Rights

  • You have the right to access all data stored about you in the app
  • You can delete any personal information by resetting it within the Settings
  • You can reset all app data at any time, which will clear all stored information
  • You can disable specific platforms from being tracked

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as you keep it in the app. Once you reset a username or clear all data, it is permanently deleted from your device. Cached data is automatically updated based on your notification preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you through the app or update the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.
